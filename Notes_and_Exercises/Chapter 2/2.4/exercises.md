## Q10

According to

$$
f(f^{-1}(x)) = x.
$$

Apply differentiation, we have

$$
f'(f^{-1}(x))(f^{-1})'(x) = 1.
$$

Thus,

$$
(f^{-1})'(x) = \frac{1}{f'(f^{-1}(x))}.
$$
